Book a call

Built for two very specific parent situations.

You do not need a giant service menu. You need a thoughtful conversation that helps you decide what kind of support is best for your child right now.

Path one

New or first-time parent navigating a diagnosis

You may be trying to understand what ABA even is, what settings exist, how to compare providers, and what matters most before starting services.

Path two

Your child is already in ABA, but it does not feel like a good fit

You may be second-guessing the current setting, provider communication, supervision, or overall quality of the experience and want a clearer outside perspective.

What we help you figure out

Not every child needs the same ABA setup.

Some children may benefit more from in-home services. Others may do better in a clinic or with school-based support. This consultation helps you think through the real pros, tradeoffs, and fit for your child rather than choosing based on whatever one provider sells.

What you walk away with

More clarity and better provider questions.

You should leave the conversation with a stronger understanding of what to look for, what to ask, and whether your next step should be starting, staying, switching, or slowing down to reassess.

Why this exists
Parents often do not need more noise. They need one clear conversation before making a big decision.
Guiding Steps ABA is designed to be that conversation.
Simple next step

Book the consultation that matches your situation.

Choose 30 minutes if you want a focused conversation and a fast sense of direction. Choose 60 minutes if you want more time to unpack your child’s history, current concerns, and provider options in depth.
