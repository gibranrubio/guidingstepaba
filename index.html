Questions that matter

Questions to ask providers

What happens if an RBT does not show up to session?
How many clients does each BCBA oversee?
Are sessions led by an RBT, a BCBA, or both?
How much parent involvement is expected?
Do you prioritize assent and reinforcement-based care?
Do you offer make-up days?
Can services happen in home, clinic, school, or community settings?
Do you support collaboration with OT and speech?
Can you work on daily living skills?

Questions families often have

What do I need to do to get started?
What will my child be doing all day?
How many hours are appropriate?
How many therapists will work with my child?
What happens if we go on vacation?
Should we choose home, clinic, school, or community services?
What can ABA help with and what can it not do?
What is the purpose of ABA?
Can ABA be applied across the lifespan?
